# Backus-Naur form (BNF)

## Definition
A formal notation for grammars, where productions are written ```<symbol> ::= <symbol1> "literal" ...```. Often extended with support for repetition (```*```, ```+```), optionality (```?``` or ```[]```) and alternatives (```|```).
